ReporteRs

An R package to generate Microsoft Word, Microsoft PowerPoint and HTML reports.

PowerPoint documents

Create Powerpoint presentations from R with your corporate template. Produce nice outputs for graphical, tabular and textual reporting.

More

Word documents

Create your own Word documents from R. Use your corporate template. Replace content in existing Word documents. Produce nice outputs for graphical, tabular and textual reporting.

Create your own HTML documents from R. Document will be styled with bootstrap. Produce nice outputs for graphical, tabular and textual reporting.
